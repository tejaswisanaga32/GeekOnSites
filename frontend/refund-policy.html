Refund Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

1. Satisfaction Guarantee

At GeekOnSites, we stand behind the quality of our work. If you are not satisfied with our service, we will make it right. Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent.

2. Eligible for Full Refund

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • We were unable to diagnose or fix the reported issue
  • The service was not performed as described
  • You cancel the service within 24 hours of scheduling and before our technician arrives
  • Parts or equipment ordered were not delivered as promised

3. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued when:

  • Only partial service was completed due to customer decision
  • Issue recurs within 7 days of service (we will re-service at no charge first)
  • Cancellation within 2 hours of scheduled appointment

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Service completed successfully and issue resolved
  • Physical damage caused by customer after service completion
  • Issues caused by customer misuse or negligence
  • Third-party software licenses or subscriptions
  • Opened or installed hardware components

5. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact us within 7 days of service completion
  2. Provide your service order number and reason for refund
  3. Allow 3-5 business days for our team to review
  4. Refunds processed to original payment method within 5-10 business days

6. Warranty on Repairs

All our repairs come with a 30-day warranty. If the same issue recurs within 30 days, we will fix it at no additional charge. This warranty covers labor only; parts warranty is subject to manufacturer terms.
